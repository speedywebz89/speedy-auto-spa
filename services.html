UV Coating

Posted by Service Bindery | 2023

Spot UV is the application of UV Coating to a specific area (or areas) of a printed piece rather than coating the entire surface. Spot UV is a creative way to add depth and contrast through varying levels of texture. We also offer flood UV coating.

Perfect Binding

Posted by Service Bindery | 2023

Perfect Binding is a widely used soft cover book binding method. With this binding method, the pages and cover are glued together at the spine with a strong thermal glue. The other three sides of the book are then trimmed as needed to give them clean "perfect" edges.

Buckle Folding

Posted by Service Bindery | 2023

Buckle folders work by feeding the paper at high speeds until it can move no further; the reaction of the paper is to buckle creating a fold. We offer a variety of different folding services, including gate folding and signature folding. Reach out to us so we can help you with your next folding project.
